Covid 19

COVID-19, the illness caused by SARS-CoV-2, emerged in late 2019 and spread very quickly across the globe. Within the first two years of the COVID-19 pandemic, more than 450 million cases were reported worldwide, more than 100 million in the EU/EEA alone.

SARS-CoV-2 is mainly spread via respiratory droplets, including aerosols, from an infected person who sneezes, coughs, speaks, sings, or breathes in close proximity to other people.Droplets can be inhaled or deposited in the nose and mouth or on the eyes.

We care for your health there for we have created a Covid-19 Survey to help you be healthy.
